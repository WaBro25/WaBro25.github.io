Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why.

I worked with both an in-office team and an offshore team. We discovered that the offshore team lacked the necessary skills to complete certain aspects of the project. This created tension within the team as no one felt comfortable addressing the issue with management. To address this, I initiated a discussion with my in-office team. We collectively decided to hold a meeting to openly discuss the situation and reach a shared understanding. In retrospect, this meeting was beneficial. It facilitated open communication and ultimately led to the replacement of some offshore developers with those possessing the required skillsets..
