What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence (EI) is the capacity to be aware of, control, and express one's emotions, and to handle interpersonal relationships judiciously and empathetically. Having the emotional awareness of ones emotions and practicing empathy on how you affect other people and taking in to account their feelings.

How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ - Emotional intelligence - The knowledge of ones feelings and emotions and how it can impact other people. Being self aware. Using empathy to understand others peoples feelings and points of view.

IQ - Mental Intelligence. Is the knowledge of things and topics and the ability to understand complex problems. Problem solving and logical reasoning.

Why is EQ important?

Because we are all different and trying to understand someone is crucial in being a person. Being able to regulate and understand ones emotions and feelings. This is healthy because a healthy mind is a healthy body
